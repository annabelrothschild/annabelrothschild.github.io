Atlanta Map Room

I worked on the first version of the Atlanta Map Room in Summer 2018 and am currently providing support to a sister location in Boston. Led by Dr. Yanni Loukissas at Georgia Tech, the goal of the Map Room is to document and reflect upon the connections and disjunctions between civic data and lived experience in the city, through the collaborative creation of large-scale, interpretive maps. Specifically, we work to contextualize personal, lived histories in Atlanta in the face of a changing city, especially in regards to gentrification. My work on the 10-week project included cleaning, processing, geocoding, and researching datasets for the data overlays, as well as work on the web app, created with JavaScript, Node.js, Socket.io, HTML and CSS, and the DataTables j-Query plugin.

A video, made by our PM, Chris Polack, describing our work can be found here.
